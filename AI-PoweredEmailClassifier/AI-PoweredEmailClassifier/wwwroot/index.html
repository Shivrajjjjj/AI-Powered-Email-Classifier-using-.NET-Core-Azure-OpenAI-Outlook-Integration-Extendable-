Welcome to AI Email Classifier

This intelligent tool helps you automatically classify incoming emails using the power of AI. Whether it's a customer support request, a sales inquiry, or spam — this classifier sorts it in seconds.

✨ What This App Can Do

  • 📨 Automatically categorize emails into Support, Sales, Urgent, Spam, and more.
  • 🧠 Uses advanced AI models like Gemini or OpenAI under the hood.
  • 📋 Keeps track of your recent classification history.
  • 📦 Extendable to Microsoft Outlook with real-time inbox scanning (via Microsoft Graph API).

🛠 How to Use

  1. Go to the Classify tab.
  2. Enter the subject and body of the email.
  3. Click Classify Email and view the result instantly.
  4. Your classifications are saved below for quick access.
